Quick help

Gameplay basics

Build a 5-card poker hand from the 9 cards on your face of the cube.

  • Hold cards you want to keep.
  • Stand to score your hand.
  • New Hand deals a fresh set of cards.
  • Max Bet wagers 100 credits instead of 5.

Subscriptions

Joker Cube uses Apple subscriptions for billing and access.

  • Manage or cancel in Apple ID Subscriptions.
  • Use Restore if access isn't detected.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple.

Troubleshooting

Most issues can be resolved quickly with a reset.

  • Force-quit and relaunch the app.
  • Confirm you're signed in to the correct Apple ID.
  • Install the latest iOS updates.

Performance

For smoother gameplay, keep background apps to a minimum.

  • Close heavy apps before playing.
  • Restart your device if animations lag.
  • Let us know your device model if issues persist.

Frequently asked questions

My subscription isn't recognized. What should I do?

Confirm you are signed in with the same Apple ID used to purchase. Tap “Restore” on the paywall, then relaunch the app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Visit apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions and choose Joker Cube to cancel.

Where is my free trial information stored?

Your free trial start date is stored securely in the device Keychain and never leaves your device.

Can I request a refund?

Yes. Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to submit a request.

What details should I include in a support email?

Please include your device model, iOS version, a brief description of the issue, and screenshots if possible.
