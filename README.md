Quick Help

1) Gameplay basics
- Build a 5-card poker hand from the 9 cards on your face of the cube.
- Hold cards you want to keep.
- Stand to score your hand.
- New Hand deals a fresh set of cards.
- Max Bet wagers 100 credits instead of 5.

2) Subscriptions
- Joker Cube uses Apple subscriptions for billing and access.
- Manage or cancel in Apple ID Subscriptions.
- Use Restore if access isn't detected.
- Refunds are handled by Apple.

3) Troubleshooting
- Most issues can be resolved quickly with a reset.
- Force-quit and relaunch the app.
- Confirm you're signed in to the correct Apple ID.
- Install the latest iOS updates.

4) Performance
- For smoother gameplay, keep background apps to a minimum.
- Close heavy apps before playing.
- Restart your device if animations lag.
- Let us know your device model if issues persist.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: My subscription isn't recognized. What should I do?
A: Confirm you are signed in with the same Apple ID used to purchase. Tap Restore on the paywall, then relaunch the app.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Visit https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions and choose Joker Cube to cancel.

Q: Where is my free trial information stored?
A: Your free trial start date is stored securely in the device Keychain and never leaves your device.

Q: Can I request a refund?
A: Yes. Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com to submit a request.

Q: What details should I include in a support email?
A: Include your device model, iOS version, a brief description of the issue, and screenshots if possible.
